# PERF-651: Replace `CaptureLoop` runWorker Dynamic Promise Await with Conditional Promise Chaining

## Focus Area
`packages/renderer/src/core/CaptureLoop.ts` - Frame Capture Loop hot path (`runWorker`).

## Background Research
In the current `runWorker` loop, there's a dynamic check `setTimeResult ? await setTimeResult.then(() => strategy.capture(page, time)) : await strategy.capture(page, time)`. The ternary operator combined with await and inline then creates closures and multiple microtask ticks. By removing the explicit `await` for the `setTimeResult` chain inside the `try` block and structuring the promise chain efficiently, we could avoid some dynamic Promise-resolution wrapper overhead generated by V8 inside the hot loop.
